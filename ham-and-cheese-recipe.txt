Ham and Cheese Sandwich

Ingredients:
  2 slices of bread
  2 slices of cheese (swiss or american cheddar)
  2 slices of ham (smoked or cooked)

Directions:
  Take the two slices of bread and lay them next to each other on a plate.
  Add a single slice of cheese to each slice of bread.
  Add a single slice of ham to each slice of bread.
  Now place the two slices of bread together.
  Eat and enjoy.

Optional:
  Add mustard, mayonnaise or pesto for some added flavor.
  Add salt and pepper for a mild flavor enhancement.
